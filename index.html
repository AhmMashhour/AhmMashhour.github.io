Profile

I am Ahmed Mashhour, a highly motivated and detail-oriented Cybersecurity Engineer with a strong foundation in incident response, threat analysis, and preventive security measures. My goal is to safeguard the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of digital assets in today's ever-evolving threat landscape.

I hold a B.Sc. in Electronics and Electrical Communication Engineering from the High Institute for Engineering & Technology in Zagazig, with a GPA of 3.1 (Very Good). I have acquired extensive experience in network security, system administration, and threat management through my work at El Sharqiya Chemicals, where I implement and enforce security policies and manage network segmentation to enhance internal security.

I continuously seek to improve my skills and knowledge through certifications and training programs, including:

  • Google - Cybersecurity Professional Certificate
  • University of London - Cyber Security: Technology and Governance Specialization
  • University of Michigan - Leading People and Teams
  • Cisco - CCNA
  • ISC2 Certified in Cybersecurity
  • LEARNKARTS - Certified Ethical Hacking (V12)

I am passionate about staying up-to-date with the latest cybersecurity trends and technologies, and I am eager to bring my analytical skills, technical expertise, and strong problem-solving abilities to a dynamic and challenging work environment.

Skills & Tools

Skills

  • Security Incident Response: Managing and responding to cybersecurity incidents in real-time, mitigating threats, and preventing future attacks.
  • Security Monitoring: Continuous monitoring of networks and systems to detect and respond to security breaches.
  • Governance, Risk Management, and Compliance (GRC): Establishing security policies and ensuring compliance with regulations.
  • Cybercrime Analysis: Investigating and analyzing cybercrime activities to prevent and mitigate cyber threats.
  • Network Security: Securing network infrastructure through firewalls, VPNs, and IDS/IPS systems.
  • INFOSEC: Implementing measures to ensure information confidentiality, integrity, and availability.
  • Vulnerability Assessment: Identifying security weaknesses in systems and networks to prevent unauthorized access.

Tools

  • Windows Server: Managing and securing Windows-based server environments.
  • Linux: Administration and securing Linux servers for enterprise environments.
  • Python: Writing automation scripts and security tools using Python.
  • SQL: Querying and securing databases using SQL for data integrity.
  • Bash scripting: Automating tasks in Unix-based systems using Bash.
  • Splunk: Collecting and analyzing machine data for real-time security monitoring.
  • QRadar: Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) tool used for threat detection and response.
  • SOAR: Security Orchestration, Automation, and Response for automating incident response tasks.
  • Wireshark: Network packet analyzer for troubleshooting and security analysis.
  • tcpdump: Command-line packet analyzer used for capturing and analyzing network traffic.
  • Suricata: Network security monitoring and intrusion detection tool.
